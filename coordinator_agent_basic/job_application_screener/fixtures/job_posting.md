# Senior Backend Engineer

**Location:** Remote (US)
**Level:** Senior (IC4)

## Must-have qualifications

- 5+ years professional software development
- Strong Python (production services, not scripts only)
- Experience building and operating distributed systems (queues, async workers, service boundaries)
- PostgreSQL or comparable relational databases in production
- Clear written communication for design docs and incident reviews

## Nice-to-have

- Kubernetes, Terraform, or AWS
- On-call experience for customer-facing APIs

## Role summary

You will own backend services for our hiring platform: screening pipelines, webhooks, and audit logging. We expect you to lead small projects, mentor mid-level engineers, and partner with product on tradeoffs.
